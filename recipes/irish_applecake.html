Irish Apple Cake

Description

This Irish apple cake has a delicate texture and is topped with a cinnamon-oat streusel and cinnamon glaze. A must for St. Patrick's Day or easy enough for an anytime snack.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • rolled oats
  • ground cinnamon
  • unsalted butter
